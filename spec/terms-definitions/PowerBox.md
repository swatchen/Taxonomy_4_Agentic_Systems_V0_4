[[def: PowerBox]]

~An architectural pattern in which the Workspace acts as the central authority for resources, managing hybrid key exchanges and "minting" the specific capability tokens used by Workflows to invoke actuators

**Principal-** A human or organization anchor that controls one or more **Agent Roles**, administers one or more **Workspaces**, and authors/curates/selects **Workflows** and **Agentflows**; governance and accountability attach through the relationship.
